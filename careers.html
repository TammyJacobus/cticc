Student Placement Programmes

The CTICC offers academically deserving students studying relevant courses in event and hospitality management the opportunity to do a sex-month internship while they are studying.

Graduate Programmes

The CTICC offers a full year's employment contract so that graduates can gain valuable work experience after they have graduated. Graduates are exposed to all operational areas of the business in order to obtain a comprehensive grounding in facilities and event management.
