Supply Chain Management Process
step 1

Register on the CTICC Supplier Database

RFQ documents can be downloaded directly from our website, free of charge.

Alternatively, RFQ documents can be purchased directly from the CTICC at a non-refundable fee of R 200 per document. These may be collected directly from the supply chain office as of the dates indicated above.

step 2

Submit your Bidding Documents

The completed original bid documents must be placed in a sealed A4 envelope – clearly stating the bid number and name of tender.

The sealed bids must be deposited into Tender box 1 situated at the main reception on the ground floor of CTICC 1.

To ensure that bids are not invalidated, bid documents must be completed in accordance with the terms and conditions stated on them.
