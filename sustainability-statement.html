Sustainability Statement

The Cape Town International Convention Centre (CTICC) strives to support the implementation of events in a sustainable manner that benefits both the society in which it operates, and the environment as a whole, by following a triple-bottom-line approach to sustainability: People Planet and Profit.

Sustainability Statement

Sustainabilityis the very core of the CTICC. It is the golden thread that runs through every aspect of the centre: its business dealings, staff empowerment, community interactions, and care for the environment.

As part of a larger community, the CTICC is committed to finding long-term, sustainabile approaches to conducting business and addressing social issues in the Western Cape and the country at large.

Economic Growth and Job Creation

Designed and built to connect Cape Town to the country, the centre aims to bring people together through world-class events that promote economic growth and job creation.

In addition, rigorous annual assessments have tracked the progress of the CTICC's economic contribution over the years. Since its opening, the CTICC has cumulatively created more than 115 000 jobs and has made a combined cumulative contribution to the regional and national economies of moe than R40 billion.

The CTICC is also aligned to the United Nations Global Compact (UNGC) principles of human rights, labour, environment and anti-corruption.

Nurture Our World

As sustainability is both a moral obligation and a strategic imperative, the CTICC plaes great importance on integrating economic, social, and environmental sustainability into every aspect of its operations. This triple-bottom-line approach is informed by a comprehensive Environmental Policy and monitored by a dedicated Sustainability Committee referred to as the NOW team.

The CTICC established its NOW initiative in 2009. Initially set up as a way to transform commitment into practical action, NOW quickly evolved into a central component of the centre's sustainability strategy. The NOW team, comprising representatives from all areas of the business, work together to ensure that sustainability stays at the centre of every aspect of the CTICC.

The work of the NOW team is complemented by a dedicated CTICC Sustainability Officer, who can be contacted by emailing NOW@cticc.co.za
