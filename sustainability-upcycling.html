The Cape Town International Convention Centre (CTICC) strives to support the implementation of events in a sustainable manner that benefits both the society in which it operates, and the environment as a whole, by following a triple-bottom-line approach to sustainability: People Planet and Profit.

Green Your Event

There are various measures event organisers can take to ensure sustainability at events:

  • Book one of our environment sustainable conference packages.

  • Measure the waste and energy of your event.

  • Choose efficient waste removal and recyling.

  • Help to uplift and empower communities.

  • Conserve water.

  • Measure and offset the carbon footprint of your event.
