Visitor Parking

Parking at the CTICC is never a problem, with over 1 400 secure,
easily accessible on-site parking bays available to visitors.

P1 is accessed via the Buitengracht or Walter Sisulu Avenue/Long Street entrance and requires a short walk across Convention Square to enter the CTICC 1. Get directions

Parking Rates

Guests are required to use the convenient pay-on-foot parking ticket kiosks to pay for their parking on an hourly basis. Alternatively, event organisers can organise to pre-book parking for their guests by prior arrangement.

Please contact the event organiser or require at one of our reception desks to find our if pre-booked or refundable parking is available for he event you are attending.
